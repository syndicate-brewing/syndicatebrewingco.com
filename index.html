  • Robust Porter

    6.3% ABV

    A stronger, more bitter version of a traditional porter. This Robust Porter has a noticeable burnt toasty bitterness that stands out due to a liberal dose of black patent malt. This helps in balancing the perceived bitterness of the aggressive hopping that is consistent with the style. Dry hopped with cascade hops for aromatic complexities.

  • Victory Lap Amber Ale

    5.3% ABV

    Woody, nutty, biscuit flavors dominate this well balanced American amber ale. The use of Victory malt gives this beer a malty complexity while allowing a slight hop presence to shine through.

  • Kolsch Rules Everything Around Me

    5% ABV

    German Hallertau hops coupled with yeast cultured from Inland Island, a Colorado company, lead to this unique Kölsch. Crisp, clean, with a fruity aroma all balance to create this easy drinking Kölsch.

  • Kyky SMASH Pale Ale

    6.3% ABV

    SMASH (single malt and single hop) Pale Ale. Brewed exclusively with Maris Otter malt and Cascade hops to showcase the characteristics of each ingredient. Hazy light orange, biscuit, nutty, floral, citrus, light grapefruit. A very mild, easy drinking pale ale with a slightly dry finish.
